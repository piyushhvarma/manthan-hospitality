About This Resort

Serenity in the Pench buffers, close to the safari gate.

Gajraj Resort Pench sits on a tranquil patch of land in the wildlife buffer zone, reached by a quiet lane just minutes from the Turia gate. The cottages and rooms are designed to offer a peaceful, nature-connected stay, where lush forest vibes surround you.

Here, life moves with wild rhythms: morning bird tours, afternoon safari drives, and delicious garden buffet dining under the stars. It is an unhurried luxury retreat for nature lovers looking to disconnect from the city.
