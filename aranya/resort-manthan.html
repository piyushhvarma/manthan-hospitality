About This Resort

Spacious lawns, designed for wildlife groups.

Manthan Resort Pench sits just a short drive from the Turiya and Khawasa gates of Pench National Park. Featuring a layout of comfortable AC Cottages, standard cottages, and rooms, the resort acts as a perfect base camp for your tiger safaris. Sprawling lawns allow families and wildlife groups to gather and relax in nature between safari drives.

Our desk is ready to help you with safari coordinates to ensure you have smooth entries to the park. Enjoy pool dips under the sky, indoor-outdoor games like badmintons or carrom, and dining that serves delicious multi-cuisine plates tailored to your taste.
