What There Is To Do

Days that shape themselves.

Every experience is hosted, not scripted, led by people who've spent years in these landscapes, offered differently at each resort.

Manthan & Gajraj

Jungle Safaris & Tiger Trails

Guided open jeep safaris in Pench Tiger Reserve, led by experienced forest guides. Explore core zones like Turia, Karmajhiri, or Jamtara, tracking tigers, leopards, and diverse birdlife. Book safari permits in advance through our travel desk.

Manthan Resort

Lawn Bonfire & Games

Spacious green lawns hosting bonfire evenings under the stars. Perfect for families to swap safari stories, enjoy local acoustic music, or participate in indoor-outdoor board games.

Gajraj Resort

Buffer Zone Nature Trails

Guided bird watching walks through peaceful buffer trails, butterfly garden exploration, and village pottery tours to Pachdhar village led by our naturalist.

Both Resorts

Chef's Table Dining

A single nightly table built around whatever came in that day: forest produce at Manthan, local village items at Gajraj. Booked directly with the kitchen, not the front desk.

Both Resorts

Spa & Wellness

Treatments drawing on regional herbs and technique to cool down and soothe you after dusty open safari drives, in open-air treatment rooms built into each landscape.
