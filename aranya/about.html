Our Story

A collection built one landscape at a time.

The Philosophy

The line that runs through every property.

Manthan means deep reflection. We started with a simple rule: a resort should read like a contour line drawn onto its landscape, as a buffer zone elevation in the forest, never a structure imposed on top of it.

That rule shapes everything from where a building sits to how many keys a property is allowed to hold. We deliberately stay small. Every Manthan Hospitality address is capped under 25 rooms, sourced from local material, and staffed largely from the surrounding villages.

We're not trying to be everywhere. We're trying to get each wilderness destination exactly right, and let the landscape do most of the talking.

Our Approach

What guides each decision we make.

Small on purpose

Every property stays under 25 rooms. It's a harder economic model, and the only one that keeps the experience feeling personal rather than managed.

Hired from the landscape

Most of our team (naturalists, boat captains, cooks, gardeners) come from the villages immediately around each resort, not from hospitality schools in distant cities.
